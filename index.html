ABOUT THE UNIVERSITY

Benjamin University (BU) is a private university located in Bukkasa, Mayuge District, Uganda. The University was founded in 2019 and was launched by H.E Jesca (Urbano), the Vice President of the Republic of Uganda on 1st November 2019.

UNIVERSITY PHILOSOPHY

Benjamin University helps students to answer the question of “after university, what is next?” by embracing Entrepreneurship, ICT, Research, and Community Empowerment in every degree or diploma course offered. Therefore, our graduates are usually well-equipped enough to create their own jobs.

MISSION

To produce graduates who are self-reliant and able to create jobs for themselves and others through quality education that blends Entrepreneurship, ICT, Research, and ethical values.

VISION

“To be a leading ICT and Entrepreneurship institution of Higher Learning in Africa and beyond.”

CORE VALUES

  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Integrity
  • Commitment to Excellence
  • Professionalism
  • Teamwork

WHAT MAKES BU UNIQUE?

BU is unique because in every degree or diploma course we emphasize Entrepreneurship, ICT, Research paper writing, and Community Empowerment in order to produce graduates who are self-reliant and ready to answer the question “after university, what’s next?”.

BU produces job creators through our annual entrepreneurship competition (Mr. & Miss Job Creators) who are able to create jobs for others.

BU is the only University whose headquarters are located in Mayuge Division, 40 km from the heart of Kampala and Jinja.
